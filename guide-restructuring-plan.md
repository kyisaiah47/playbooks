# Guide Restructuring Plan - Wikipedia × Notion Transformation

**Goal**: Transform from 1,298 guides to ~980 high-quality, life-planning focused guides.

**Key Changes**:
- Career/Work: 538 → ~130 guides (cut generic job guides, keep transitions & situations + edgier topics)
- Relationships: 24 → ~150 guides (6x expansion - including controversial topics)
- Finance: 19 → ~80 guides (4x expansion)
- Life Events: 300 → ~300 guides (adding edgy modern challenges)
- Health/Wellness: 271 → ~250 guides (reframe medical to "living with")
- Personal Growth: 146 → ~100 guides (cut abstract, keep tangible skills)

**Wikipedia-Level Coverage**: Now includes controversial/taboo topics that other platforms avoid.
