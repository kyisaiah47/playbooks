CATEGORY 1: Getting Started & Education
1. Research the basics of blockchain technology and how cryptocurrencies work.
2. Learn about different types of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoins.
3. Understand the difference between hot wallets, cold wallets, and exchange wallets.
4. Study common crypto terminology like DeFi, staking, yield farming, and market cap.
5. Read about major crypto exchanges and their security features.
6. Learn how to read crypto charts and basic technical analysis.
7. Understand the tax implications of cryptocurrency trading in your jurisdiction.
8. Research dollar-cost averaging and other investment strategies for crypto.
9. Learn about crypto security best practices and common scams to avoid.
10. Set up your first crypto wallet and practice sending small test transactions.

CATEGORY 2: Portfolio Planning & Strategy
1. Define your investment goals and risk tolerance for cryptocurrency investing.
2. Determine what percentage of your total investment portfolio should be crypto.
3. Research and select 3-5 cryptocurrencies for your initial portfolio allocation.
4. Create a diversification strategy across different crypto sectors and market caps.
5. Set up a dollar-cost averaging schedule for regular crypto purchases.
6. Establish clear entry and exit price targets for each cryptocurrency position.
7. Plan your portfolio rebalancing schedule and criteria for adjustments.
8. Research staking opportunities for passive income on your crypto holdings.
9. Set up a system to track your portfolio performance and compare to benchmarks.
10. Create a written investment policy statement for your crypto portfolio decisions.

CATEGORY 3: Security & Storage
1. Set up a hardware wallet for long-term storage of your cryptocurrency holdings.
2. Create secure backup phrases for all your wallets and store them safely offline.
3. Enable two-factor authentication on all cryptocurrency exchange accounts.
4. Research and implement a multi-signature wallet setup for large holdings.
5. Create a secure system for storing and organizing your private keys.
6. Set up withdrawal whitelists and spending limits on your exchange accounts.
7. Research crypto insurance options to protect your digital asset holdings.
8. Create an emergency access plan for your crypto in case of incapacitation.
9. Regularly audit your wallet security settings and update passwords.
10. Test your backup and recovery procedures with small amounts of crypto.

CATEGORY 4: Trading & Execution
1. Choose a reputable cryptocurrency exchange that meets your trading needs.
2. Complete the KYC verification process and set up bank account linking.
3. Start with small purchases to get comfortable with the trading interface.
4. Set up recurring buy orders for your dollar-cost averaging strategy.
5. Learn to use limit orders, stop-losses, and other advanced order types.
6. Research and execute your first cryptocurrency swap or DeFi transaction.
7. Set up price alerts for your target cryptocurrencies and market conditions.
8. Practice using different order types during various market conditions.
9. Research and try out decentralized exchanges for certain trading pairs.
10. Document all your trades for tax reporting and performance analysis.

CATEGORY 5: Monitoring & Management
1. Set up a portfolio tracking app to monitor your crypto holdings in real-time.
2. Create a monthly review schedule to assess portfolio performance and rebalance.
3. Track important crypto news and regulatory developments affecting your holdings.
4. Monitor staking rewards and DeFi yields to optimize passive income opportunities.
5. Set up automated tax reporting tools to track capital gains and losses.
6. Create alerts for significant price movements or portfolio value changes.
7. Research and evaluate new cryptocurrency opportunities for potential addition.
8. Review and adjust your risk management strategy based on market conditions.
9. Track your portfolio's performance against relevant crypto market benchmarks.
10. Document lessons learned and refine your crypto investment strategy over time.
