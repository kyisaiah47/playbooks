CATEGORY 1: Business Foundation & Setup
1. Define your coaching niche and target audience in one clear sentence.
2. Research and choose your business structure (LLC, sole proprietorship, etc.).
3. Register your business name and secure matching domain and social media handles.
4. Open a dedicated business bank account separate from personal finances.
5. Create a simple business plan outlining your services, pricing, and goals.
6. Set up basic accounting software or system to track income and expenses.
7. Obtain necessary business licenses and permits for your location.
8. Design a professional logo and basic brand identity elements.
9. Write your coaching philosophy and mission statement in 2-3 sentences.
10. Create a dedicated workspace in your home or rent office space.

CATEGORY 2: Coaching Skills & Credentials
1. Enroll in a reputable coach training program to gain foundational skills.
2. Practice active listening techniques with friends or family members daily.
3. Learn and master at least 5 different coaching models and frameworks.
4. Complete required training hours for your chosen coaching certification.
5. Find a mentor coach to guide your professional development.
6. Join a coaching practice group to refine your skills with peers.
7. Record and review your coaching sessions to identify improvement areas.
8. Study your niche area deeply through books, courses, and research.
9. Develop your own coaching toolkit with assessments and exercises.
10. Schedule regular supervision sessions with an experienced coach.

CATEGORY 3: Marketing & Client Acquisition
1. Create a professional website with clear descriptions of your coaching services.
2. Write and publish weekly blog posts or articles about your coaching specialty.
3. Develop a lead magnet (free resource) to capture potential client contact information.
4. Network at local business events and introduce yourself as a coach.
5. Ask satisfied clients for referrals and testimonials you can use in marketing.
6. Create profiles on relevant social media platforms and post valuable content regularly.
7. Offer free discovery sessions to potential clients to demonstrate your value.
8. Partner with complementary businesses that serve your target audience.
9. Speak at local organizations or events about topics in your coaching area.
10. Develop a clear elevator pitch that explains what you do in 30 seconds.

CATEGORY 4: Service Delivery & Client Experience
1. Design a structured onboarding process for new coaching clients.
2. Create intake forms and assessments to understand each client's needs.
3. Develop session templates and agendas to ensure consistent coaching delivery.
4. Set up a reliable video conferencing system for virtual coaching sessions.
5. Create a client portal or system for sharing resources and tracking progress.
6. Establish clear boundaries and expectations with clients from the start.
7. Develop follow-up procedures to check on client progress between sessions.
8. Create a library of resources, worksheets, and tools for different client situations.
9. Design a system for collecting client feedback and measuring coaching outcomes.
10. Plan celebration rituals for when clients achieve their coaching goals.

CATEGORY 5: Business Growth & Sustainability
1. Track your monthly revenue, expenses, and profit margins consistently.
2. Raise your coaching rates annually as you gain experience and results.
3. Develop group coaching programs to serve more clients simultaneously.
4. Create passive income streams like online courses or digital products.
5. Build an email list and send regular valuable content to your subscribers.
6. Hire a virtual assistant to handle administrative tasks as you grow.
7. Develop strategic partnerships with other coaches or service providers.
8. Set aside 10% of revenue for professional development and business growth.
9. Create systems and processes that allow your business to run without constant oversight.
10. Plan quarterly business reviews to assess progress and adjust strategies.
