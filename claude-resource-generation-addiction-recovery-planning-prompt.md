### Practical Value Metrics

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would addiction professionals and recovery specialists recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to addiction treatment and recovery support professionals?
- Are treatment recommendations and recovery strategies current and evidence-based?
- Does this solve real challenges individuals face in addiction recovery planning and maintenance?
- Would this prevent common recovery mistakes while optimizing treatment outcomes and long-term sobriety?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic addiction information available in countless recovery websites and self-help resources
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring individual addiction patterns and recovery needs
- Oversimplified recovery advice that doesn't address the complexity of substance use disorders
- Generic sobriety tips that don't reflect professional treatment and evidence-based recovery practices

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based addiction recovery planning with current treatment knowledge and recovery frameworks
- Individual-centered approaches that address specific substances, usage patterns, and personal circumstances
- Comprehensive treatment navigation that optimizes access while ensuring appropriate care matching
- Recovery-focused planning that includes relapse prevention and long-term maintenance strategies
- Support system development that promotes sustainable recovery and community integration

## Success Validation for Addiction Recovery Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Clinical Standards**: Would addiction professionals and recovery specialists endorse this guidance?
2. **Recovery Effectiveness**: Does this optimize treatment outcomes and long-term sobriety maintenance?
3. **Safety Priority**: Does this ensure appropriate medical management and crisis intervention protocols?
4. **Individual Adaptation**: Does this accommodate different addiction patterns and recovery approaches?
5. **Community Integration**: Does this promote sustainable recovery through support system development?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this addiction recovery topic, would this guide them to develop an effective, evidence-based approach to recovery that addresses their specific needs while building sustainable sobriety and community support systems?
