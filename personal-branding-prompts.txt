CATEGORY 1: Foundation & Self-Discovery
1. Write down your top 5 core values and how they guide your decisions.
2. List 10 unique skills or experiences that make you different from others in your field.
3. Ask 5 trusted colleagues to describe your greatest professional strengths in one sentence.
4. Identify the specific problem you solve for people or organizations.
5. Write a 50-word description of who you are and what you stand for.
6. Review your past achievements and identify 3 recurring themes or patterns.
7. Define your ideal audience or the type of people you want to influence.
8. Create a personal mission statement that captures your professional purpose.
9. List 5 topics you could talk about for hours without preparation.
10. Document 3 career moments when you felt most authentic and energized.

CATEGORY 2: Visual Identity & Online Presence
1. Choose 3 colors that represent your personality and use them consistently across platforms.
2. Take or commission a professional headshot that reflects your industry and personality.
3. Write a compelling LinkedIn headline that goes beyond just your job title.
4. Create a personal logo or visual mark that you can use across all materials.
5. Audit all your social media profiles and remove anything that doesn't align with your brand.
6. Design a simple email signature that includes your key contact information and value proposition.
7. Choose 2-3 fonts that you'll use consistently in all your written materials.
8. Create a personal website or landing page with your bio, contact info, and portfolio.
9. Develop a consistent photo style or filter for your social media posts.
10. Write compelling "About" sections for all your professional platforms using the same core message.

CATEGORY 3: Content Creation & Thought Leadership
1. Write one LinkedIn post sharing a professional insight or lesson learned.
2. Start a weekly newsletter or blog sharing industry trends and personal perspectives.
3. Record a 2-minute video introducing yourself and your expertise.
4. Share a behind-the-scenes post showing your work process or daily routine.
5. Write a case study detailing how you solved a specific problem for a client or employer.
6. Create an infographic or visual guide related to your area of expertise.
7. Post a "lessons learned" reflection after completing a major project.
8. Share a book recommendation with 3 key takeaways that relate to your field.
9. Write a contrarian opinion piece about a common practice in your industry.
10. Create a "day in the life" post showing what you actually do at work.

CATEGORY 4: Networking & Relationship Building
1. Reach out to 5 people in your industry to set up informal coffee chats.
2. Comment thoughtfully on 10 posts from people you'd like to connect with professionally.
3. Attend one industry event or conference and introduce yourself to 3 new people.
4. Send personalized LinkedIn connection requests to 10 relevant professionals each month.
5. Join 2 professional groups or communities where your target audience spends time.
6. Offer to help someone in your network solve a problem they've posted about.
7. Share and add commentary to content created by people in your professional circle.
8. Host a virtual coffee chat or webinar on a topic you're passionate about.
9. Write genuine recommendations for 3 colleagues or connections on LinkedIn.
10. Follow up with new connections within 48 hours of meeting them with a personalized message.

CATEGORY 5: Reputation Management & Growth
1. Set up Google Alerts for your name to monitor your online reputation.
2. Ask satisfied clients or colleagues to write recommendations or testimonials.
3. Apply to speak at one industry conference or local meetup about your expertise.
4. Guest post on a relevant blog or contribute to an industry publication.
5. Create a simple portfolio showcasing your best work with brief explanations.
6. Join a podcast as a guest to share your expertise with a new audience.
7. Respond professionally and promptly to all comments and messages on your content.
8. Track which types of content get the most engagement and create more of that.
9. Set aside 30 minutes weekly to engage with your professional community online.
10. Create a simple system to collect and organize positive feedback you receive.
