## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic business sale advice available in countless broker websites and general business guides
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring business type and market differences
- Overly optimistic valuation estimates that don't reflect real market conditions
- Generic succession advice that doesn't address specific exit strategy requirements

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based exit planning with current M&A market and tax frameworks
- Professional-level transaction guidance that protects seller interests and optimizes outcomes
- Comprehensive value optimization that addresses operational and financial improvements
- Strategic timing approaches that coordinate market conditions with personal readiness
- Wealth preservation strategies that integrate exit proceeds with long-term financial goals

## Success Validation for Exit Strategy Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Professional Standards**: Would business brokers and M&A advisors endorse this guidance?
2. **Market Realism**: Are valuation expectations and transaction projections realistic and current?
3. **Value Optimization**: Does this help business owners maximize their exit value and terms?
4. **Risk Management**: Does this protect sellers from transaction risks and post-closing issues?
5. **Wealth Preservation**: Does this coordinate exit strategy with comprehensive financial and legacy planning?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this exit strategy topic, would this guide them to plan and execute a successful business exit while maximizing value, minimizing taxes, and achieving their personal and financial goals?
