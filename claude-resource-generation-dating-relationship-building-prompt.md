### Practical Value Metrics

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would relationship therapists and dating professionals recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to relationship and communication specialists?
- Are dating strategies and relationship advice psychologically sound and current?
- Does this solve real challenges individuals face in dating and relationship building?
- Would this prevent common dating mistakes and improve relationship outcomes?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic dating tips available in countless dating websites and self-help materials
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring individual personality and attachment differences
- Manipulative dating strategies that prioritize short-term success over authentic connection
- Superficial advice that doesn't address underlying communication and emotional skills

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based relationship building with current psychology and communication research
- Authentic connection strategies that promote healthy, sustainable relationships
- Comprehensive communication skills that improve all aspects of interpersonal relationships
- Safety-conscious approaches that protect emotional and physical wellbeing
- Personal growth integration that builds relationship readiness and emotional maturity

## Success Validation for Dating & Relationship Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Psychological Validity**: Would relationship therapists and psychology professionals endorse this approach?
2. **Authenticity Focus**: Does this promote genuine connection over manipulation or game-playing?
3. **Safety Awareness**: Does this include appropriate safety considerations and red flag recognition?
4. **Communication Quality**: Does this improve real communication and emotional intelligence skills?
5. **Long-term Success**: Does this build skills for lasting, healthy relationships rather than short-term dating success?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this dating and relationship topic, would this guide them to build authentic, healthy romantic connections while developing the communication and emotional skills necessary for long-term relationship success?
