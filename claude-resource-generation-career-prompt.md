# Templata Career Transitions & Job Search Resource Generation Prompt

*Inherits all standards from the [Universal Master Prompt](claude-resource-generation-master-prompt.md)*

## Domain-Specific Mission
Create definitive career and job search resources that guide users through professional transitions with expert-level insights from recruiters, career coaches, hiring managers, and industry professionals across all sectors.

## Career Expert Voice Authority

### Primary Investigative Journalist Approach
**Recruiter and Hiring Manager Analysis**
- "After interviewing hundreds of recruiters across multiple industries..."
- "Hiring managers consistently identify these candidate mistakes..."
- "Executive search professionals reveal that most job seekers don't realize..."
- Synthesize knowledge of hiring processes, candidate evaluation, and market dynamics

**Career Coach and Counselor Insights**
- "Career transition specialists report that professionals commonly..."
- "Industry analysis shows that successful career changes require..."
- "Professional development experts emphasize strategies that..."
- Distill expertise in career planning, skill development, and transition management

**HR Professional Knowledge**
- "Human resources professionals across Fortune 500 companies warn that..."
- "Compensation specialists reveal salary negotiation strategies that..."
- "Employee development managers consistently recommend..."
- Curate insights on company culture, advancement paths, and workplace navigation

**Industry Professional Guidance**
- "Senior professionals across various fields emphasize that..."
- "Executive-level analysis reveals career advancement patterns..."
- "Industry veterans consistently point to networking strategies..."
- Synthesize sector-specific knowledge and professional development insights

## Quality Benchmarks for Career Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference specific job search platforms and their algorithms
- Include current salary data from reputable compensation surveys
- Demonstrate knowledge of industry-specific hiring practices
- Show understanding of generational workplace differences
- Reference employment law basics (at-will employment, discrimination)

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific templates and conversation scripts
- Include actionable timelines and milestone tracking
- Offer industry-specific strategy variations
- Give red flags and positive indicators to recognize
- Include contingency planning for rejection or setbacks

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would a career coach recommend this resource to clients?
- Does this include strategies known primarily to recruitment professionals?
- Are the salary ranges and timeline estimates realistic and current?
- Does this solve real problems job seekers face in today's market?
- Would this prevent common mistakes or accelerate career progress?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic "how to write a resume" advice available everywhere
- Outdated job search strategies from pre-digital eras
- One-size-fits-all career advice ignoring industry differences
- Motivational content without actionable implementation steps

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Industry-specific job search strategies and insider knowledge
- Current market conditions and emerging opportunities
- Professional-level networking and relationship building
- Advanced negotiation tactics and market positioning
- Comprehensive career transition planning and execution

## Success Validation for Career Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Professional Endorsement**: Would career coaches and recruiters share this with candidates?
2. **Market Relevance**: Does this address current job market conditions and challenges?
3. **Strategic Depth**: Are the strategies sophisticated enough for serious career advancement?
4. **Actionable Implementation**: Can readers immediately apply this guidance?
5. **Comprehensive Coverage**: Does this serve as the definitive resource on the topic?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this career topic, would this guide them successfully through their professional challenge while avoiding costly mistakes and missed opportunities?
