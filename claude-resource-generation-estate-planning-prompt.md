## Quality Benchmarks for Estate Planning Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current estate and tax law changes and planning opportunities
- Include state-specific considerations and legal requirement variations
- Demonstrate knowledge of various planning tools and their appropriate applications
- Show understanding of family dynamics and communication challenges
- Reference reputable estate planning organizations and continuing education standards

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific document checklists and legal requirement guidance
- Include actionable family communication strategies and conversation scripts
- Offer decision frameworks for trust selection and planning tool evaluation
- Give realistic cost estimates and professional selection guidance
- Include maintenance schedules and update triggers for estate plans

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would estate planning attorneys and wealth management professionals recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to estate planning specialists?
- Are the legal and tax strategies current and appropriately qualified?
- Does this solve real challenges families face in legacy planning?
- Would this prevent common estate planning mistakes and family conflicts?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic will templates available in countless legal websites and software
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring family dynamics and state law variations
- Overly complex strategies without explanation of simpler alternatives
- Legal advice that should require professional consultation and customization

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based planning strategies with current legal and tax frameworks
- Family-centered approaches that address communication and relationship dynamics
- Comprehensive coordination strategies that integrate all aspects of financial life
- Professional guidance for selecting and working with estate planning teams
- Practical implementation approaches that balance complexity with accessibility

## Success Validation for Estate Planning Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Legal Standards**: Would estate planning attorneys and trust professionals endorse this?
2. **Tax Accuracy**: Are tax strategies and implications current and correctly explained?
3. **Family Focus**: Does this address real family dynamics and communication challenges?
4. **Professional Integration**: Does this guide appropriate use of professional services?
5. **Practical Implementation**: Can individuals realistically take action based on this guidance?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this estate planning topic, would this guide them to create a comprehensive legacy plan that protects their family, minimizes taxes, and prevents conflicts while ensuring proper legal compliance?
