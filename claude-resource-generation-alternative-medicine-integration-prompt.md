**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Evidence Standards**: Would integrative medicine specialists and evidence-based healthcare professionals endorse this guidance?
2. **Safety Priority**: Does this ensure appropriate risk assessment and safety protocol implementation?
3. **Professional Integration**: Does this facilitate effective healthcare team coordination and communication?
4. **Quality Assurance**: Does this help individuals identify qualified practitioners and evidence-based treatments?
5. **Patient Empowerment**: Does this enable informed decision-making while maintaining healthcare safety standards?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this alternative medicine topic, would this guide them to safely and effectively integrate complementary therapies with conventional healthcare while ensuring professional coordination and evidence-based decision-making?
