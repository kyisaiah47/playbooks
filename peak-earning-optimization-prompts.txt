CATEGORY 1: Skill Development & Value Creation
1. Identify the top 3 skills most valued in your industry and create a learning plan for each.
2. Complete one professional certification or online course every quarter to stay current.
3. Document your expertise by writing articles or creating content in your field.
4. Seek feedback from managers and peers to identify specific skills gaps.
5. Practice public speaking to improve your ability to communicate value.
6. Learn complementary skills that make you more versatile within your role.
7. Join professional associations and attend industry conferences regularly.
8. Find a mentor who earns at the level you aspire to reach.
9. Take on stretch assignments that showcase your capabilities to leadership.
10. Build technical skills that can automate parts of your current work.

CATEGORY 2: Career Strategy & Positioning
1. Research salary ranges for your role across different companies and regions.
2. Create a personal brand strategy that highlights your unique value proposition.
3. Build a portfolio showcasing your best work and measurable achievements.
4. Network with professionals in higher-paying roles or companies.
5. Schedule quarterly career conversations with your manager about advancement.
6. Apply to at least three new opportunities every six months to benchmark your value.
7. Negotiate your current compensation using market data and performance metrics.
8. Identify companies known for paying above-market rates in your field.
9. Consider relocating to higher-paying geographic markets if feasible.
10. Explore leadership or management tracks that typically offer higher compensation.

CATEGORY 3: Income Diversification & Side Ventures
1. Launch a consulting practice offering services in your area of expertise.
2. Create and sell an online course teaching skills you've mastered.
3. Develop a passive income stream through affiliate marketing or partnerships.
4. Invest time in building a personal brand that can generate speaking opportunities.
5. Write and self-publish a book or guide related to your professional expertise.
6. Start a blog or newsletter that can be monetized through sponsorships.
7. Offer freelance services during evenings or weekends in your specialty.
8. Create digital products or tools that solve problems in your industry.
9. Explore revenue-sharing opportunities or equity compensation in startups.
10. Build a social media presence that can attract brand partnerships.

CATEGORY 4: Financial Optimization & Investment
1. Maximize contributions to all available tax-advantaged retirement accounts.
2. Negotiate equity compensation or stock options as part of your total package.
3. Invest in index funds or diversified portfolios to grow wealth long-term.
4. Create an emergency fund covering 6-12 months of expenses before taking risks.
5. Use tax-loss harvesting and other strategies to minimize your tax burden.
6. Consider real estate investment as a hedge against inflation and income source.
7. Automate your savings to ensure consistent wealth building regardless of income fluctuations.
8. Work with a financial advisor to optimize your investment strategy for high earners.
9. Explore tax-efficient ways to convert income into long-term capital gains.
10. Track your net worth monthly and set specific financial milestones.

CATEGORY 5: Performance & Results Optimization
1. Set measurable goals tied directly to revenue or business outcomes.
2. Track and document all your professional achievements with specific metrics.
3. Proactively communicate your wins and impact to key stakeholders.
4. Streamline your workflow to focus on the highest-value activities.
5. Delegate or eliminate tasks that don't directly contribute to your earning potential.
6. Build systems and processes that make your work more scalable and efficient.
7. Seek roles or projects with clear performance incentives and bonus structures.
8. Create accountability partnerships with colleagues to maintain high performance.
9. Regularly review and optimize how you spend your working hours.
10. Develop a personal board of advisors who can guide your career decisions.
