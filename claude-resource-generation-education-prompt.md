# Templata Education & Learning Planning Resource Generation Prompt

*Inherits all standards from the [Universal Master Prompt](claude-resource-generation-master-prompt.md)*

## Domain-Specific Mission
Create definitive education and learning resources that guide students and families through major educational decisions with expert-level insights from educators, admissions professionals, learning specialists, and career development experts.

## Quality Benchmarks for Education Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current educational research and pedagogical best practices
- Include realistic admissions statistics and financial aid data
- Demonstrate knowledge of learning differences and accommodation strategies
- Show understanding of career market demands and skill requirements
- Reference reputable educational organizations and accreditation standards

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific implementation timelines and milestone tracking
- Include cost-benefit analysis for educational investments
- Offer decision frameworks for complex educational choices
- Give actionable study strategies and learning techniques
- Include resource lists and support service directories

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would educators and admissions professionals recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to educational professionals?
- Are the academic timelines and preparation strategies realistic?
- Does this solve real educational challenges students and families face?
- Would this prevent costly mistakes or improve learning outcomes?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic study tips available in countless academic success guides
- One-size-fits-all educational advice ignoring individual learning differences
- Outdated college admissions strategies from pre-digital application processes
- Unrealistic educational promises or guaranteed success formulas

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based learning strategies with current educational research
- Professional-level guidance for complex educational decisions
- Comprehensive planning that addresses multiple learning scenarios
- Financial literacy for educational investments and career planning
- Inclusive approaches that support diverse learning needs and backgrounds

## Success Validation for Education Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Educational Standards**: Would teachers, counselors, and administrators endorse this?
2. **Learning Science**: Are recommendations supported by current educational research?
3. **Practical Application**: Can students and families implement these strategies effectively?
4. **Financial Responsibility**: Does this promote sound educational investment decisions?
5. **Inclusive Support**: Does this address diverse learning needs and educational pathways?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this educational topic, would this guide them to make informed decisions that optimize their learning outcomes and educational investment while supporting their long-term career goals?
