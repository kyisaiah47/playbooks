## Quality Benchmarks for Mental Health Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current mental health treatment guidelines and evidence-based practices
- Include insurance parity laws and access advocacy information
- Demonstrate knowledge of various therapy modalities and their effectiveness
- Show understanding of crisis intervention and emergency mental health services
- Reference reputable mental health organizations and professional standards

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific assessment tools and treatment planning guides
- Include actionable provider selection criteria and evaluation frameworks
- Offer decision frameworks for treatment modality selection and goal setting
- Give realistic timeline expectations and progress measurement guidance
- Include resource directories for crisis services and community support

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would mental health professionals and treatment coordinators recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to mental health treatment specialists?
- Are treatment recommendations and navigation strategies current and appropriate?
- Does this solve real challenges individuals face in accessing and coordinating mental healthcare?
- Would this prevent common treatment mistakes and improve mental health outcomes?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic mental health information available in countless wellness websites and apps
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring individual symptoms and treatment needs
- Diagnostic information that should require professional evaluation
- Crisis advice that substitutes for professional emergency intervention

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based treatment planning with current clinical and insurance frameworks
- Professional coordination strategies that optimize treatment effectiveness and accessibility
- Recovery-focused approaches that promote long-term mental wellness and resilience
- Advocacy guidance that helps individuals navigate mental health systems and insurance barriers
- Family-centered approaches that support both patients and their support systems

## Success Validation for Mental Health Treatment Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Clinical Standards**: Would mental health professionals and treatment coordinators endorse this guidance?
2. **Evidence-Based**: Are treatment recommendations supported by current clinical research and best practices?
3. **Access-Focused**: Does this help individuals overcome real barriers to mental healthcare access?
4. **Recovery-Oriented**: Does this promote long-term mental wellness and sustainable recovery?
5. **Safety-Conscious**: Does this include appropriate crisis planning and professional intervention guidance?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this mental health treatment topic, would this guide them to access appropriate professional care, coordinate effective treatment, and build the support systems necessary for mental wellness and recovery?
