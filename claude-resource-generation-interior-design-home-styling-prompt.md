- Generic design trends without consideration of personal style and budget realities

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based interior design with current space planning expertise and aesthetic principles

- Comprehensive design planning that balances aesthetics with functionality and budget
- Accessibility-aware design that promotes inclusive and adaptable living spaces
- Sustainability-conscious choices that consider environmental impact and long-term value

## Success Validation for Interior Design and Home Styling Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Design Expertise**: Would professional interior designers and space planners endorse this guidance?
2. **Functional Excellence**: Does this optimize space utility while maintaining aesthetic appeal?
3. **Lifestyle Integration**: Does this accommodate real family living and individual needs?
4. **Budget Awareness**: Does this provide realistic guidance for various budget levels and investment priorities?
5. **Long-term Value**: Does this promote design decisions that enhance both livability and home value?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this interior design and home styling topic, would this guide them to create functional, beautiful living spaces that reflect their personal style while accommodating their lifestyle needs and budget constraints?
