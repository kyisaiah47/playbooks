CATEGORY 1: Content Strategy & Planning
1. Define your brand voice and tone guidelines in a single document.
2. Research and list 20 trending hashtags relevant to your industry.
3. Create a content calendar template for the next 30 days.
4. Identify your target audience demographics and pain points.
5. Analyze three competitors' social media strategies and note key insights.
6. Establish posting frequency goals for each social platform you use.
7. Create templates for different post types (promotional, educational, behind-the-scenes).
8. Set up a content idea repository using a spreadsheet or note-taking app.
9. Define your key performance indicators (KPIs) for social media success.
10. Schedule a weekly content planning session in your calendar.

CATEGORY 2: Content Creation & Production
1. Take 50 high-quality photos of your products, workspace, or team.
2. Write 20 engaging captions that align with your brand voice.
3. Design 10 branded templates for quote posts and announcements.
4. Create a library of stock photos that match your brand aesthetic.
5. Record 5 short videos showcasing your expertise or behind-the-scenes moments.
6. Develop 10 different call-to-action phrases for various post objectives.
7. Create branded graphics for common post types like tips or testimonials.
8. Write evergreen content pieces that can be reused throughout the year.
9. Batch create content for one week during a dedicated creation session.
10. Develop a style guide for consistent visual branding across all posts.

CATEGORY 3: Platform Management & Optimization
1. Complete and optimize your bio sections on all active social platforms.
2. Set up professional business accounts on your chosen social media platforms.
3. Create and save Instagram Story highlight covers that match your brand.
4. Configure notification settings to respond to comments and messages promptly.
5. Link your social media accounts to your website and email list.
6. Set up social media management tools like Buffer, Hootsuite, or Later.
7. Create saved reply templates for common customer questions or comments.
8. Organize your content into themed folders for easy access and scheduling.
9. Set up tracking pixels for social media advertising if you plan to run ads.
10. Configure cross-posting settings to share content efficiently across platforms.

CATEGORY 4: Community Engagement & Growth
1. Follow and genuinely engage with 20 accounts in your industry daily.
2. Respond to all comments on your posts within 4 hours.
3. Share and comment meaningfully on posts from your followers and peers.
4. Join three relevant Facebook groups or LinkedIn communities in your niche.
5. Host a live Q&A session to directly connect with your audience.
6. Create and run a simple contest or giveaway to boost engagement.
7. Collaborate with one complementary business or influencer each month.
8. Share user-generated content and always credit the original creator.
9. Send personalized messages to new followers welcoming them to your community.
10. Create shareable content that encourages your audience to tag friends.

CATEGORY 5: Analytics & Performance Tracking
1. Set up Google Analytics to track social media traffic to your website.
2. Review your social media insights weekly to identify top-performing content.
3. Track your follower growth rate across all platforms monthly.
4. Monitor mentions of your brand or business name across social media.
5. Create a simple spreadsheet to log your monthly social media metrics.
6. Identify your best posting times by analyzing when your audience is most active.
7. A/B test different caption styles to see what resonates with your audience.
8. Set up Google Alerts for your brand name and relevant industry keywords.
9. Calculate your engagement rate and compare it to industry benchmarks.
10. Schedule monthly reviews to assess progress toward your social media goals.
