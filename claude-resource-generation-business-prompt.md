# Templata Business Launch & Entrepreneurship Resource Generation Prompt

*Inherits all standards from the [Universal Master Prompt](claude-resource-generation-master-prompt.md)*

## Domain-Specific Mission
Create definitive business launch and entrepreneurship resources that guide aspiring entrepreneurs through major business decisions with expert-level insights from successful business owners, startup advisors, legal professionals, and venture capital specialists.

**Business Operations and Advisory Guidance**

- "Experienced business mentors consistently identify patterns where..."
- Synthesize expertise in operations, marketing, team building, and scaling strategies

## Quality Benchmarks for Business Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current business regulations and tax implications
- Include realistic startup statistics and success/failure rates
- Demonstrate knowledge of funding landscapes and investor requirements
- Show understanding of industry-specific challenges and opportunities
- Reference reputable business research and entrepreneurship studies

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific implementation frameworks and step-by-step guides
- Include cost estimates and timeline expectations for business milestones
- Offer decision matrices for complex business choices
- Give actionable templates and resource lists
- Include case study analysis and lessons learned from real businesses

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would experienced entrepreneurs and business advisors recommend this?
- Does this include insights known primarily to business professionals?
- Are the financial projections and timelines realistic and conservative?
- Does this solve real challenges that entrepreneurs face?
- Would this prevent costly mistakes or accelerate business success?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic "how to start a business" advice available everywhere
- Get-rich-quick business schemes or unrealistic success promises
- One-size-fits-all business advice ignoring industry differences
- Outdated business strategies from pre-digital business environments

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based business planning with current market data
- Legal and financial strategies that minimize risk and optimize outcomes
- Professional-level analysis of complex business decisions
- Industry-specific guidance that addresses real market conditions
- Comprehensive planning that addresses multiple business scenarios

## Success Validation for Business Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Professional Standards**: Would successful entrepreneurs and business advisors endorse this?
2. **Financial Realism**: Are cost estimates, timelines, and projections conservative and accurate?
3. **Legal Compliance**: Does this reflect current business law and regulatory requirements?
4. **Market Relevance**: Does this address current business environment and challenges?
5. **Practical Implementation**: Can entrepreneurs execute these strategies immediately?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this business topic, would this guide them to make informed decisions that increase their probability of business success while avoiding costly mistakes and legal problems?
