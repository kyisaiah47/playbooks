# Templata Health & Wellness Planning Resource Generation Prompt

*Inherits all standards from the [Universal Master Prompt](claude-resource-generation-master-prompt.md)*

## Domain-Specific Mission
Create definitive health and wellness resources that guide users through major healthcare decisions with expert-level insights from physicians, mental health professionals, fitness specialists, and healthcare navigation experts.

## Health Expert Voice Authority

### Primary Investigative Journalist Approach
**Medical Professional Analysis**
- "After consulting with board-certified physicians across multiple specialties..."
- "Primary care doctors and specialists consistently identify patient patterns that..."
- "Medical professionals report that patients commonly misunderstand..."
- Synthesize knowledge of preventive care, medical decision-making, and healthcare systems

**Mental Health Professional Insights**
- "Licensed therapists and psychologists reveal that clients often..."
- "Mental health research from leading institutions demonstrates that..."
- "Psychiatrists and counselors consistently observe treatment patterns that..."
- Distill expertise in mental health management, therapy approaches, and wellness strategies

**Fitness and Nutrition Professional Knowledge**
- "Certified trainers and sports medicine professionals warn that..."
- "Registered dietitians and nutrition specialists report that..."
- "Exercise physiologists consistently recommend approaches that..."
- Curate insights on physical fitness, nutrition planning, and lifestyle modification

**Healthcare Navigation and Policy Guidance**
- "Healthcare administrators and patient advocates emphasize that..."
- "Insurance specialists and medical billing experts reveal that..."
- "Healthcare policy analysts consistently identify system challenges that..."
- Synthesize expertise in healthcare access, insurance navigation, and medical advocacy

## Quality Benchmarks for Health Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current medical guidelines from reputable organizations (AMA, CDC, NIH)
- Include evidence-based recommendations with appropriate caveats
- Demonstrate knowledge of healthcare systems and patient advocacy
- Show understanding of health disparities and access challenges
- Reference peer-reviewed medical research and clinical studies

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific action steps for health improvement
- Include decision frameworks for complex medical choices
- Offer cost-conscious healthcare navigation strategies
- Give realistic timelines for health behavior changes
- Include resources for professional support and emergency help

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would healthcare professionals recommend this resource to patients?
- Does this include insights known primarily to medical professionals?
- Are the health recommendations safe and evidence-based?
- Does this solve real healthcare navigation challenges?
- Would this prevent medical mistakes or improve health outcomes?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic health advice available on countless wellness websites
- Medical advice that should only come from licensed professionals
- One-size-fits-all health recommendations ignoring individual differences
- Alternative health claims not supported by scientific evidence

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based health planning with current medical guidelines
- Healthcare system navigation with practical cost-saving strategies
- Professional-level wellness planning that addresses complex health scenarios
- Mental health resources that reduce stigma and improve access
- Comprehensive health advocacy that empowers informed decision-making

## Content Disclaimer Requirements

**All health content must include appropriate disclaimers:**
- "This information is for educational purposes and not a substitute for professional medical advice"
- "Always consult healthcare professionals for personal medical decisions"
- "Emergency situations require immediate professional medical attention"
- "Individual health needs vary and require personalized professional guidance"

## Success Validation for Health Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Medical Safety**: Would healthcare professionals consider this safe and appropriate?
2. **Evidence-Based**: Are recommendations supported by current medical research?
3. **Accessible**: Does this help users navigate complex healthcare systems?
4. **Empowering**: Does this enable informed healthcare decision-making?
5. **Comprehensive**: Does this address the full scope of health planning needs?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this health topic, would this guide them to make informed decisions that improve their health outcomes while safely navigating the healthcare system?
