CATEGORY 1: Research and Inspiration
1. Browse travel blogs and social media for destination ideas that match your interests.
2. Create a Pinterest board or mood board with photos of places you want to visit.
3. Research the best time of year to visit your potential destinations.
4. Read recent traveler reviews on TripAdvisor or Google Reviews for honest insights.
5. Watch YouTube travel vlogs to get a feel for the destination's atmosphere.
6. Check visa requirements and passport validity for your target countries.
7. Research local customs, culture, and etiquette to avoid cultural missteps.
8. Look up major attractions, landmarks, and must-see experiences in each destination.
9. Investigate potential health risks and required vaccinations for your destinations.
10. Create a shortlist of 3-5 destinations that fit your budget and timeline.

CATEGORY 2: Budget Planning and Financial Preparation
1. Set a realistic total budget including flights, accommodation, food, activities, and emergencies.
2. Research average daily costs for food, transportation, and activities in your destination.
3. Compare flight prices across different airlines and booking platforms.
4. Look into accommodation options from budget hostels to luxury hotels.
5. Notify your bank and credit card companies about your travel dates and destinations.
6. Research the local currency and current exchange rates.
7. Set up a dedicated travel savings account and start depositing money regularly.
8. Look into travel insurance options and purchase appropriate coverage.
9. Research whether tipping is customary and budget accordingly.
10. Download currency conversion and expense tracking apps for your phone.

CATEGORY 3: Booking and Reservations
1. Book your flights at least 6-8 weeks in advance for better prices.
2. Reserve accommodation that fits your budget and location preferences.
3. Book popular attractions, tours, or restaurants that require advance reservations.
4. Arrange airport transfers or research public transportation options from the airport.
5. Book rental cars if needed and research local driving requirements.
6. Make restaurant reservations for special dining experiences you don't want to miss.
7. Purchase tickets for shows, concerts, or special events happening during your visit.
8. Book guided tours for complex or historically significant sites.
9. Reserve spa treatments, adventure activities, or unique experiences in advance.
10. Print and organize all confirmation emails and booking references.

CATEGORY 4: Practical Preparation and Packing
1. Check your passport expiration date and renew if it expires within 6 months.
2. Apply for necessary visas and ensure you have all required documentation.
3. Create a packing checklist based on your destination's climate and planned activities.
4. Pack versatile clothing items that can be mixed, matched, and layered.
5. Organize important documents in a travel folder including copies stored separately.
6. Download offline maps and translation apps to your smartphone.
7. Pack a basic first aid kit with essential medications and health supplies.
8. Arrange for mail hold, plant care, or pet sitting while you're away.
9. Charge all electronic devices and pack appropriate chargers and adapters.
10. Leave detailed itinerary and emergency contact information with family or friends.

CATEGORY 5: On-Trip Navigation and Experience
1. Check in for flights 24 hours before departure and download boarding passes.
2. Arrive at the airport with plenty of time for security and potential delays.
3. Keep digital and physical copies of important documents easily accessible.
4. Stay flexible with your daily schedule to allow for spontaneous discoveries.
5. Try local cuisine and ask locals for their favorite restaurant recommendations.
6. Take photos throughout your trip but also take time to experience moments without a camera.
7. Keep track of your expenses to stay within budget and adjust spending as needed.
8. Learn basic phrases in the local language to enhance interactions with locals.
9. Stay safe by keeping valuables secure and being aware of your surroundings.
10. Document your favorite experiences and places for future trip planning reference.
