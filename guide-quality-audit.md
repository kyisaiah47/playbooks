# Guide Quality Audit - Wikipedia × Notion Perspective

**Context**: Templata is "Wikipedia × Notion for life planning" - comprehensive coverage with practical workspaces.

**Total Guides**: 1,298
- Career/Work: 538 (41%)
- Life Events: 300 (23%)
- Health/Wellness: 271 (21%)
- Personal Growth: 146 (11%)
- Relationships: 24 (2%)
- Finance: 19 (1%)

---

## Overall Assessment

### The Good
✅ **Comprehensive career coverage** - Every niche job imaginable
✅ **Diverse life situations** - From mundane to edge cases
✅ **Practical categories** - Aligns with what people actually search for

### The Bad
❌ **Way too career-heavy** - 41% is just job paths (boring, commoditized content)
❌ **Weak on relationships** - Only 24 guides? This is a MAJOR life area
❌ **Weak on finance** - Only 19 guides? People desperately need this
❌ **Generic naming** - Everything is "[X] Career" or "[Y] Skills"

### The Ugly
🚩 **This doesn't feel like Wikipedia × Notion** - It feels like a job board crossed with a self-help blog
🚩 **Career guides are commoditized** - Every career site has "How to become a [job]"
🚩 **Missing depth** - No controversial topics, no nuanced situations, no real edge

---

## Category-by-Category Audit

### Career/Work (538 guides) - BLOATED ⚠️

**What works:**
- "Literary Agent Search" - Specific, actionable
- "Management Role Transition" - Actual life moment
- "Executive Promotion" - Real situation people face
- "Transgender Workplace Transition" - Underserved, important
- "Mid-40s Career Pivot" - Life stage, not job hunting

**What doesn't:**
- "Zookeeper Career" - Who is searching for this?
- "Carpet Installer Career" - Extremely niche
- "Cement Mason Career" - Not planning, just job description
- "Casino Dealer Career" - Low search volume
- Every single "[Job Title] Career" guide - These are all the same template

**Verdict:**
- Keep: ~100 guides for major careers and career transitions
- Cut: 400+ generic job guides
- Add: Career situations (toxic boss, burnout, imposter syndrome, salary negotiation)

---

### Life Events (300 guides) - PROMISING ✅

**What works:**
- "Wedding" - Core life event
- "Multi-Generational Household" - Real modern challenge
- "Coming Out" - Important, underserved
- "Quarter Life Crisis" - Specific life moment
- "Lottery Winning Financial Adjustment" - Edge case but interesting
- "Swinging Lifestyle" - Bold, specific
- "Friends With Benefits" - Real situation people navigate

**What doesn't:**
- "Digital Calendar" - This is a tool, not a life event
- "Password Management" - System, not event
- "Public Transit Navigation" - Too basic/mundane
- "Vehicle Emergency Kit" - Prep task, not planning

**Verdict:**
- These are actually good - keep most
- Add more: Job loss, empty nest, becoming an influencer, cancel culture

---

### Health/Wellness (271 guides) - MEDICAL OVERLOAD 🏥

**What works:**
- "Menopause" - Major life transition
- "Major Surgery Recovery" - Specific moment
- "Sleep Hygiene" - Practical, actionable
- "Martial Arts Training" - Skill development
- "Climate Anxiety" - Modern, relevant

**What doesn't:**
- "Somatic Symptom Disorder" - Too clinical
- "Specific Phobia Treatment" - Medical, not planning
- "Separation Anxiety Disorder" - Diagnosis, not guide
- "Illness Anxiety Disorder" - These should be "coping with X" not "X disorder"

**Verdict:**
- Reframe medical conditions as "Living with [X]" or "Managing [X]"
- Less diagnosis, more daily coping
- Add: Grief, body image, aging anxiety

## What's Missing (Wikipedia × Notion Gap)

If this were truly "Wikipedia for life," you'd have:

### Controversial/Taboo Topics
- Infidelity recovery
- Abortion decision-making
- Estrangement from family
- Sex work transition
- Religious deconversion
- Political activism
- Cancel culture survival

### Modern Life Challenges
- Social media detox (you have internet addiction, but not conscious reduction)
- Remote work boundaries
- AI job displacement
- Gig economy survival
- ADHD in adults (diagnosis & coping)
- Dating app fatigue

### Nuanced Situations
- Being the other woman/man
- Favoritism among your kids
- Regretting parenthood
- Career vs family tradeoffs
- Firing a friend
- Dealing with racist relatives

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Massive Career Purge
Cut 70% of niche job guides. Keep only:
- Top 50 careers by search volume
- Career transitions (not jobs)
- Workplace situations (not roles)

### 2. 5x Relationship Content
Go from 24 → 120 guides
Focus on: Dating, marriage challenges, family conflict, friendship

### 3. 4x Finance Content
Go from 19 → 80 guides
Focus on: Debt, savings, major purchases, financial transitions

### 4. Rename Everything
Stop with "[X] Career" and "[Y] Skills"
Use specific, searchable, human language

### 5. Get Edgier
Wikipedia covers everything, even uncomfortable topics
You're playing it too safe

---

## Bottom Line

**Current vibe:** LinkedIn Learning meets WebMD
**Target vibe:** Wikipedia meets Notion

You need less "how to become a zookeeper" and more "how to navigate your husband's affair" or "deciding whether to have kids at 40."

The career bloat is killing the positioning. This should feel like a comprehensive life encyclopedia, not a job board.
