CATEGORY 1: Communication & Connection
1. Schedule a daily video call at the same time each day to create routine.
2. Send a good morning text with something you're looking forward to sharing with them.
3. Write a handwritten letter and mail it to their address.
4. Set up a shared photo album where you both add pictures throughout the week.
5. Plan a virtual date night with a movie you both watch simultaneously.
6. Send voice messages instead of texts when you have something important to say.
7. Create a shared playlist and add songs that remind you of each other.
8. Play an online game together for 30 minutes this evening.
9. Record a video message explaining why you appreciate them today.
10. Share three things that happened in your day during your next conversation.

CATEGORY 2: Trust & Security Building
1. Be transparent about your daily schedule and who you're spending time with.
2. Follow through on every small promise you make this week.
3. Address a concern or doubt you've been having instead of letting it fester.
4. Share a personal insecurity or fear you haven't mentioned before.
5. Ask them directly about something that's been bothering you rather than assuming.
6. Introduce your partner to a new friend via video call or social media.
7. Give them access to your location when you're out for the evening.
8. Discuss and agree on social media boundaries that work for both of you.
9. Share your calendar for the upcoming week so they know when you're free.
10. Talk openly about past relationship experiences that still affect you.

CATEGORY 3: Visit Planning & Logistics
1. Set a specific date for your next visit and book the travel arrangements today.
2. Research and pick three activities you want to do together during the visit.
3. Calculate the total cost of visits for this year and create a savings plan.
4. Apply for a travel rewards credit card to reduce future flight costs.
5. Create a shared calendar marking all planned visits for the next six months.
6. Look into alternative transportation options that might be cheaper than flying.
7. Discuss and decide who will visit whom for the next three trips.
8. Find someone who can watch your pet or water your plants during visits.
9. Research the best times to book flights for maximum savings to their location.
10. Plan what you'll cook together during your next visit and make a grocery list.

CATEGORY 4: Future Planning & Goals
1. Discuss a specific timeline for when one of you will relocate permanently.
2. Research job opportunities in each other's cities and share what you find.
3. Create a joint savings account specifically for closing the distance.
4. Make a pros and cons list for each possible city you could live together.
5. Set a monthly savings goal that brings you closer to being together.
6. Research the cost of living differences between your two locations.
7. Discuss what type of living situation you want when you move in together.
8. Create a shared Pinterest board with ideas for your future home together.
9. Make a list of non-negotiable factors that will influence your final location choice.
10. Set up a video call with a real estate agent in your target city.

CATEGORY 5: Personal Growth & Independence
1. Join a new hobby or class that gives you something interesting to share.
2. Maintain plans with friends even when your partner is available to video chat.
3. Set a personal goal unrelated to your relationship and work toward it this week.
4. Practice expressing your needs clearly without being passive or aggressive.
5. Spend a weekend doing activities you enjoy without constant contact with your partner.
6. Read a book about relationships or personal development and discuss insights with them.
7. Exercise or do something physical that makes you feel confident and strong.
8. Learn a new skill that you can eventually teach or share with your partner.
9. Address a personal issue or bad habit instead of using the relationship as distraction.
10. Make peace with a family member or friend to reduce stress in your life.
