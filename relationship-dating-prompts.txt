CATEGORY 1: Self-Preparation & Readiness
1. Write down your core values and what you're looking for in a partner.
2. Update your wardrobe with clothes that make you feel confident and authentic.
3. Practice good hygiene and grooming habits daily.
4. Work on one personal hobby or interest that brings you joy.
5. Set healthy boundaries around your time and energy.
6. Address any unresolved issues from past relationships through reflection or therapy.
7. Create a comfortable living space that you'd be proud to show someone.
8. Build a support network of friends and family who encourage your growth.
9. Develop financial stability and independence in your life.
10. Practice self-compassion and positive self-talk about your dating journey.

CATEGORY 2: Meeting & Initial Connection
1. Join activities or groups where you can meet like-minded people naturally.
2. Create an authentic dating profile that showcases your genuine personality.
3. Practice making eye contact and smiling when meeting new people.
4. Ask open-ended questions to learn about someone's interests and values.
5. Listen actively without immediately thinking about your response.
6. Share something genuine about yourself early in conversations.
7. Suggest a simple, low-pressure activity for a first meeting.
8. Follow up within 24-48 hours if you're interested in seeing someone again.
9. Be clear about your intentions and what type of relationship you're seeking.
10. Trust your instincts about whether someone feels like a good match.

CATEGORY 3: Early Dating & Getting to Know Each Other
1. Plan dates that allow for conversation and getting to know each other better.
2. Share stories about your family, childhood, and formative experiences.
3. Discuss your future goals and aspirations openly.
4. Observe how they treat service workers, friends, and family members.
5. Notice whether they respect your time, boundaries, and opinions.
6. Be honest about your past relationships and what you learned from them.
7. Pay attention to how conflicts or disagreements are handled between you.
8. Introduce them to your friends and see how everyone gets along.
9. Have conversations about deal-breakers and non-negotiables in relationships.
10. Take time apart to reflect on your feelings and compatibility.

CATEGORY 4: Building Deeper Connection & Intimacy
1. Share your fears, insecurities, and vulnerabilities with each other.
2. Create meaningful traditions or rituals that are unique to your relationship.
3. Support each other through challenges and celebrate successes together.
4. Have honest conversations about physical intimacy and boundaries.
5. Meet each other's families and important people in your lives.
6. Discuss your communication styles and how to resolve conflicts constructively.
7. Talk about your individual needs for space and togetherness.
8. Plan future experiences or trips that you both look forward to.
9. Practice forgiveness and work through disagreements with empathy.
10. Express gratitude regularly for what you appreciate about each other.

CATEGORY 5: Commitment & Long-Term Planning
1. Have explicit conversations about whether you want an exclusive relationship.
2. Discuss your timeline for major relationship milestones like moving in together.
3. Talk openly about your views on marriage, children, and family planning.
4. Create shared financial goals and discuss how you'll handle money together.
5. Plan where you want to live and how you'll balance career aspirations.
6. Discuss how you'll maintain individual identities within the relationship.
7. Talk about how you'll handle relationships with ex-partners and opposite-sex friends.
8. Create agreements about household responsibilities and daily life logistics.
9. Discuss how you'll support each other's personal growth and individual goals.
10. Make concrete plans for your future together and revisit them regularly.
