CATEGORY 1: Product Discovery & Research
1. Browse the homepage to see featured products and current promotions.
2. Use the search bar to find specific products you're looking for.
3. Filter products by price range to stay within your budget.
4. Sort products by customer ratings to find the highest-rated items.
5. Read product descriptions carefully to understand features and specifications.
6. Check product availability and estimated delivery times.
7. View multiple product images and zoom in to see details.
8. Compare similar products side-by-side to make informed decisions.
9. Read customer reviews to learn about real user experiences.
10. Add items to your wishlist to save them for later consideration.

CATEGORY 2: Product Evaluation & Selection
1. Check the product's return policy before making a purchase decision.
2. Verify the seller's reputation and customer service ratings.
3. Look for product warranties or guarantees offered by the store.
4. Calculate total costs including shipping, taxes, and any additional fees.
5. Check if there are any current discount codes or coupons available.
6. Verify product compatibility with your existing items or requirements.
7. Review the product's size chart or dimensions to ensure proper fit.
8. Check if the product is eligible for free shipping or expedited delivery.
9. Look for bundle deals or related products that might save you money.
10. Screenshot or bookmark the product page to reference later.

CATEGORY 3: Account Management & Security
1. Create a new account with a strong, unique password.
2. Verify your email address to activate your account fully.
3. Add your shipping address to your account profile.
4. Save your preferred payment methods securely in your account.
5. Set up two-factor authentication for enhanced account security.
6. Update your personal information and contact details as needed.
7. Review and adjust your email notification preferences.
8. Check your order history to track past purchases.
9. Update your password regularly to maintain account security.
10. Log out of your account when using shared or public devices.

CATEGORY 4: Checkout & Payment
1. Review all items in your shopping cart before proceeding to checkout.
2. Apply any available discount codes or promotional offers.
3. Select your preferred shipping method and delivery speed.
4. Choose your payment method from saved options or add a new one.
5. Double-check your shipping address for accuracy and completeness.
6. Review the final total including all taxes and fees.
7. Read and accept the terms and conditions before completing your order.
8. Take a screenshot of your order confirmation for your records.
9. Check your email for order confirmation and tracking information.
10. Save your receipt and order number for future reference.
