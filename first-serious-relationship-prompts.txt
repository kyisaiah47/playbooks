CATEGORY 1: Communication & Understanding
1. Have an honest conversation about your relationship expectations and goals.
2. Practice active listening by summarizing what your partner says before responding.
3. Discuss your communication styles and how you prefer to handle disagreements.
4. Share something vulnerable about yourself to deepen emotional intimacy.
5. Ask your partner about their love languages and share yours.
6. Schedule regular check-ins to discuss how the relationship is going.
7. Learn to express your needs clearly without being demanding.
8. Practice giving constructive feedback when issues arise.
9. Discuss your boundaries and respect your partner's boundaries.
10. Create safe words or signals for when conversations become too heated.

CATEGORY 2: Building Trust & Commitment
1. Follow through consistently on promises you make to your partner.
2. Be transparent about your past relationships and dating history.
3. Introduce your partner to your close friends and family members.
4. Delete dating apps and make your relationship status public.
5. Share passwords for social media accounts if you both feel comfortable.
6. Discuss your timeline for major relationship milestones.
7. Be honest about your fears and insecurities in the relationship.
8. Support your partner through a difficult time or challenge.
9. Make joint decisions about social events and weekend plans.
10. Talk openly about what commitment means to each of you.

CATEGORY 3: Managing Conflict & Differences
1. Learn your partner's conflict resolution style and adapt accordingly.
2. Take a break during heated arguments to cool down and reflect.
3. Focus on specific behaviors rather than attacking your partner's character.
4. Apologize sincerely when you make mistakes or hurt your partner's feelings.
5. Find compromises on issues where you have different preferences.
6. Discuss your different backgrounds and how they shape your perspectives.
7. Agree on ground rules for fair fighting during disagreements.
8. Practice forgiveness and let go of minor grievances quickly.
9. Seek to understand your partner's viewpoint even when you disagree.
10. Consider couples counseling if conflicts become frequent or unresolved.

CATEGORY 4: Intimacy & Romance
1. Plan a special date that reflects your partner's interests and preferences.
2. Express physical affection in ways your partner enjoys most.
3. Discuss your physical intimacy preferences and boundaries openly.
4. Create new romantic traditions or rituals together as a couple.
5. Share your fantasies and desires in a safe, judgment-free environment.
6. Surprise your partner with thoughtful gestures or small gifts.
7. Make time for regular date nights without phones or distractions.
8. Write your partner a heartfelt letter expressing your feelings.
9. Try new activities together to create shared memories and experiences.
10. Maintain romance even during stressful or busy periods.

CATEGORY 5: Future Planning & Growth
1. Discuss your career goals and how they might affect the relationship.
2. Talk about your financial values and approach to money management.
3. Share your thoughts on marriage, children, and long-term commitment.
4. Plan a trip together that requires coordination and compromise.
5. Set relationship goals and check your progress regularly.
6. Discuss where you each want to live in the next few years.
7. Support each other's personal growth and individual interests.
8. Talk about how you'll handle holidays and family obligations.
9. Create a shared vision for what you want your relationship to become.
10. Make important decisions together like adopting a pet or signing a lease.
