# Templata Parenting & Child Development Resource Generation Prompt

*Inherits all standards from the [Universal Master Prompt](claude-resource-generation-master-prompt.md)*

## Domain-Specific Mission
Create definitive parenting and child development resources that guide families through major child-rearing decisions with expert-level insights from pediatricians, child psychologists, early childhood educators, and family development specialists.

**Pediatric and Child Development Analysis**

- "Board-certified pediatricians report that parents commonly..."

**Educational Professional Insights**
- "Early childhood educators and child development experts reveal that..."
- "Educational research from leading institutions demonstrates that..."
- "School administrators and teachers consistently observe that..."
- Distill expertise in learning development, educational choices, and academic preparation

**Family Therapy and Psychology Knowledge**

- "Mental health professionals specializing in children report that..."

- Curate insights on family dynamics, behavioral management, and emotional development

**Childcare and Safety Professional Guidance**
- "Childcare professionals and safety experts emphasize that..."
- "Pediatric safety specialists consistently identify hazards that..."
- "Licensed childcare providers report effective strategies for..."
- Synthesize expertise in child safety, care strategies, and family logistics

## Quality Benchmarks for Parenting Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current pediatric guidelines and research studies
- Include age-specific recommendations based on developmental science
- Demonstrate knowledge of educational best practices and learning theory
- Show understanding of family systems and child psychology
- Reference reputable parenting research and professional organizations

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific implementation strategies with age considerations
- Include troubleshooting guides for common challenges
- Offer decision frameworks for major parenting choices
- Give realistic timelines and expectation setting
- Include resources for additional support and professional help

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would pediatricians and child development specialists recommend this?
- Does this include strategies known primarily to family professionals?
- Are the developmental expectations realistic and evidence-based?
- Does this solve real parenting challenges families face?
- Would this prevent harmful mistakes or promote healthy development?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic parenting advice available in countless mommy blogs
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring individual child differences
- Judgmental content about parenting styles and family choices
- Outdated parenting strategies not supported by current research

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based parenting strategies with current developmental research
- Professional-level guidance for complex family decisions
- Comprehensive planning that addresses multiple family scenarios
- Age-specific strategies that respect developmental differences
- Inclusive approaches that support diverse family structures

## Success Validation for Parenting Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Professional Standards**: Would pediatricians and child development experts endorse this?
2. **Evidence-Based**: Are recommendations supported by current child development research?
3. **Age-Appropriate**: Does this respect developmental stages and individual differences?
4. **Practical Implementation**: Can parents execute these strategies in real family life?
5. **Family-Centered**: Does this support healthy family relationships and child wellbeing?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this parenting topic, would this guide them to make decisions that support their child's healthy development while strengthening family relationships?
