## Quality Benchmarks for Divorce Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current family law and custody standards across jurisdictions
- Include evidence-based co-parenting strategies and child welfare research
- Demonstrate knowledge of various divorce processes and their appropriate applications
- Show understanding of financial planning and asset protection during divorce
- Reference reputable family law organizations and professional standards

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific procedure checklists and timeline guidance
- Include actionable communication strategies and conflict resolution techniques
- Offer decision frameworks for legal process selection and attorney evaluation
- Give realistic cost estimates and financial planning guidance
- Include resource directories for counseling, legal aid, and support services

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would family law attorneys and divorce specialists recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to divorce and custody professionals?
- Are legal procedures and financial strategies current and jurisdiction-appropriate?
- Does this solve real challenges individuals face during divorce proceedings?
- Would this prevent common divorce mistakes and protect family wellbeing?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic divorce advice available in countless legal websites and self-help materials
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring state law variations and family circumstances
- Overly adversarial strategies that prioritize winning over family wellbeing
- Legal advice that requires professional consultation and case-specific analysis

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based divorce planning with current legal and financial frameworks
- Child-centered approaches that prioritize family welfare over individual advantage
- Comprehensive financial protection strategies that ensure post-divorce stability
- Emotional wellness guidance that supports healthy family transitions
- Professional coordination strategies that maximize positive outcomes while controlling costs

## Success Validation for Divorce Planning Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Legal Standards**: Would family law attorneys and divorce professionals endorse this guidance?
2. **Child Welfare**: Does this prioritize children's best interests and family wellbeing?
3. **Financial Protection**: Does this help individuals protect their financial future and assets?
4. **Emotional Health**: Does this support mental wellness and healthy family transitions?
5. **Practical Implementation**: Can individuals realistically navigate divorce using this guidance?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this divorce topic, would this guide them to achieve a fair resolution that protects their children, preserves their financial security, and supports their emotional recovery while minimizing conflict and cost?
