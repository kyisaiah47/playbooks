CATEGORY 5: Strategic Career Advancement
1. Identify 5 key decision-makers or influencers in your target companies and connect with them.
2. Request informational interviews with professionals in roles you aspire to hold.
3. Find a mentor who can guide your career development and networking strategy.
4. Become a mentor yourself to junior professionals in your field.
5. Join the board of directors for a nonprofit organization to expand your leadership network.
6. Attend exclusive industry events or executive networking groups if eligible.
7. Cultivate relationships with recruiters who specialize in your industry or function.
8. Build connections with journalists or bloggers who cover your industry.
9. Develop partnerships with complementary professionals who serve similar target markets.
10. Create a "power network" of 50-100 key contacts and systematically nurture these relationships.
