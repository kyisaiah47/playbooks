## Quality Benchmarks for Death & Grief Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current hospice care standards and advance directive requirements
- Include evidence-based grief counseling and bereavement support research
- Demonstrate knowledge of various cultural and religious death practices
- Show understanding of legal processes and estate settlement procedures
- Reference reputable hospice, grief counseling, and bereavement organizations

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific planning checklists and conversation guides
- Include actionable grief support strategies and healing techniques
- Offer decision frameworks for end-of-life care and funeral planning
- Give realistic timeline expectations and cost planning guidance
- Include resource directories for hospice care, counseling, and support services

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would hospice professionals and grief counselors recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to end-of-life and bereavement specialists?
- Are care recommendations and grief support strategies evidence-based and current?
- Does this solve real challenges families face during death and grief experiences?
- Would this prevent common mistakes and improve healing outcomes?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic grief advice available in countless self-help and memorial websites
- One-size-fits-all approaches ignoring cultural, religious, and individual variations
- Overly clinical information without emotional support and practical guidance
- Generic funeral planning advice without cost awareness and meaningful personalization

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based end-of-life planning with current medical and legal frameworks
- Culturally sensitive approaches that honor diverse death and mourning traditions
- Comprehensive grief support that addresses individual and family healing needs
- Professional coordination strategies that ensure quality care and meaningful ceremonies
- Practical guidance that balances emotional needs with legal and financial requirements

## Success Validation for Death & Grief Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Professional Standards**: Would hospice and grief professionals endorse these approaches?
2. **Cultural Sensitivity**: Does this respect diverse death, mourning, and memorial traditions?
3. **Emotional Support**: Does this provide genuine comfort and practical healing guidance?
4. **Family Focus**: Does this support all family members through death and grief processes?
5. **Practical Implementation**: Can individuals realistically navigate death and grief using this guidance?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this death and grief topic, would this guide them to prepare appropriately for end-of-life, create meaningful memorial experiences, and navigate grief in a way that promotes healing while honoring their loved one's memory?
