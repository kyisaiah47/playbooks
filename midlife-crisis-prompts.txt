CATEGORY 1: Self-Discovery & Reflection
1. Write down three core values that truly matter to you right now.
2. List five activities that made you feel most alive in the past year.
3. Identify one childhood dream you never pursued and explore why.
4. Take a personality test and reflect on how you've changed over the decades.
5. Write a letter to your younger self about what you've learned.
6. Create a vision board of what you want your life to look like in five years.
7. Spend 30 minutes journaling about what success means to you now.
8. Ask three close friends to describe your biggest strengths.
9. Make a list of things you're doing out of obligation versus genuine interest.
10. Schedule a solo retreat day to think about your life's direction.

CATEGORY 2: Career & Purpose Reassessment
1. Research three career paths you've always been curious about.
2. Schedule informational interviews with people in fields that interest you.
3. Take an online course in a skill you want to develop.
4. Update your LinkedIn profile to reflect your current interests and goals.
5. Volunteer for a cause you care about to explore new purpose areas.
6. Create a side project or hobby that could become income-generating.
7. Meet with a career counselor to discuss potential transitions.
8. Attend a networking event in an industry you're curious about.
9. Calculate your financial runway for making a career change.
10. Write down what you want your professional legacy to be.

CATEGORY 3: Health & Vitality Renewal
1. Schedule a comprehensive health checkup with your doctor.
2. Start a new fitness routine that you actually enjoy.
3. Experiment with a healthier eating plan for two weeks.
4. Take a dance class or learn a physical skill you've never tried.
5. Begin a daily meditation or mindfulness practice.
6. Get a sleep study done if you're having sleep issues.
7. Book a massage or spa day to prioritize self-care.
8. Try a new outdoor activity like hiking, kayaking, or cycling.
9. Join a sports league or fitness group to meet new people.
10. Create a daily routine that includes movement and stress relief.

CATEGORY 4: Relationships & Social Connections
1. Reach out to an old friend you've lost touch with.
2. Plan a meaningful conversation with your spouse or partner about your futures.
3. Join a club or group based on a hobby or interest you have.
4. Schedule regular one-on-one time with each of your children.
5. Set boundaries with relationships that drain your energy.
6. Make new friends by attending local community events.
7. Consider couples counseling to strengthen your primary relationship.
8. Plan a trip with friends to reconnect and create new memories.
9. Have honest conversations with family members about your changing needs.
10. Seek out a mentor or become a mentor to someone else.

CATEGORY 5: Adventure & New Experiences
1. Plan a solo trip to a destination you've always wanted to visit.
2. Learn a new skill that challenges you mentally, like a language or instrument.
3. Take a cooking class for a cuisine you've never tried making.
4. Sign up for an adventure activity like rock climbing or scuba diving.
5. Attend a workshop or conference completely outside your usual interests.
6. Start a creative project like painting, writing, or photography.
7. Take a weekend road trip to explore somewhere within driving distance.
8. Try a completely different style of vacation than you usually take.
9. Join a book club or discussion group to engage with new ideas.
10. Challenge yourself to say "yes" to three invitations you'd normally decline.
