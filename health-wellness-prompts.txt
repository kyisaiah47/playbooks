CATEGORY 1: Physical Movement & Exercise
1. Take a 10-minute walk around your neighborhood or office building.
2. Do 10 push-ups, squats, or jumping jacks during your lunch break.
3. Take the stairs instead of the elevator for one week.
4. Try a new physical activity like dancing, swimming, or yoga.
5. Stand up and stretch for 2 minutes every hour while working.
6. Park farther away from store entrances to add extra steps.
7. Do bodyweight exercises while watching TV for 15 minutes.
8. Join a local sports league or fitness class this month.
9. Replace one car trip per week with walking or biking.
10. Set a daily step goal and track it with your phone or fitness tracker.

CATEGORY 2: Nutrition & Hydration
1. Drink a glass of water first thing when you wake up.
2. Add one serving of vegetables to every meal for three days.
3. Replace one sugary drink with water or herbal tea today.
4. Plan and prep healthy snacks for the upcoming week.
5. Eat slowly and chew each bite thoroughly during one meal.
6. Try one new healthy recipe this week.
7. Keep a food journal for three days to track your eating patterns.
8. Fill half your plate with fruits and vegetables at dinner tonight.
9. Pack a healthy lunch instead of buying fast food tomorrow.
10. Reduce your portion size by using a smaller plate for one week.

CATEGORY 3: Mental Health & Stress Management
1. Practice deep breathing for 5 minutes when you feel stressed.
2. Write down three things you're grateful for each morning.
3. Take a 5-minute break from screens every two hours.
4. Call or text a friend or family member you haven't spoken to recently.
5. Spend 10 minutes in nature or looking out a window mindfully.
6. Practice saying "no" to one commitment that drains your energy.
7. Try a 5-minute guided meditation using a phone app.
8. Create a calming bedtime routine starting one hour before sleep.
9. Listen to your favorite music for 15 minutes without multitasking.
10. Schedule one activity this week that brings you joy.

CATEGORY 4: Sleep & Recovery
1. Go to bed 15 minutes earlier than usual tonight.
2. Put your phone in another room while you sleep for one week.
3. Keep your bedroom temperature between 65-68°F for better sleep.
4. Avoid caffeine after 2 PM for three days straight.
5. Create a consistent wake-up time and stick to it for one week.
6. Take a warm bath or shower before bedtime tonight.
7. Write down tomorrow's tasks before going to bed to clear your mind.
8. Remove electronic devices from your bedroom this week.
9. Try using blackout curtains or an eye mask for better sleep.
10. Limit daytime naps to 20 minutes or avoid them entirely.

CATEGORY 5: Preventive Health & Self-Care
1. Schedule your annual physical exam or overdue medical checkup.
2. Wash your hands thoroughly for 20 seconds after using the bathroom.
3. Apply sunscreen before going outside, even on cloudy days.
4. Schedule a dental cleaning or eye exam you've been postponing.
5. Take your prescribed medications at the same time each day.
6. Learn one fact about a health condition that runs in your family.
7. Update your emergency contact information with your doctor.
8. Practice good posture while sitting at your desk for one day.
9. Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces in your home.
10. Set a reminder to check your skin for any changes monthly.
